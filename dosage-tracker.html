Welcome to the Peptide Research Dosage Tracker

This is a private tracker that works offline — all data stays in your browser. This app saves data locally on the specific device being used.

Research organization tool only. This tracker does not provide medical advice, treatment guidance, or product recommendations.

Quick Guide:

  • Add cycles: name, dose, AM/PM, start, on length, off weeks (0 = single), days of week
  • Off weeks = 0 → single cycle | > 0 → repeating
  • Click a day → view doses (AM first), mark taken, edit/delete cycle
  • Use the Reconstitution Calculator below for mixing
  • Export current month as clean grid PDF for printing
