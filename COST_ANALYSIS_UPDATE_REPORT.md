# Health Dynamics Cost Analysis Update

Updated the Cost Analysis section to focus on health/wellness budgeting rather than product purchasing.

## Added
- Health Cost Analysis Center
- Federal benchmark reference data for 2026 Marketplace out-of-pocket limits, 2026 HSA/HDHP limits, 2026 Medicare Part B costs, and 2025 employer-sponsored premium benchmarks
- Local-only privacy disclaimer
- Editable scenario form for premiums, employer contribution, deductible, out-of-pocket max, copays, labs, prescriptions, therapy/wellness, emergency reserve, HSA/FSA, reimbursements, state/local notes, and plan notes
- Live calculations for annual premium, planned care estimate, estimated annual total, worst-case exposure, and monthly budget target
- Charts for annual scenario cost, coverage type cost, premium vs care estimate, and worst-case exposure
- CSV export for saved cost scenarios

## Safety / legal positioning
- The app does not provide legal, insurance, tax, billing, or medical advice.
- The app does not claim HIPAA, ACA, ERISA, IRS, Medicare, Medicaid, CCPA/CPRA, or state-law compliance.
- User entries remain stored locally on the user device unless exported by the user.
- Official plan documents, licensed professionals, and government sources control.
